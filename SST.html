What motivated people to travel in ancient times?
During ancient times people travelled out of their places of resi- dence for various reasons, mainly for migrant and military purposes. Other drivers included trade, spectacle games and festivity attendance, and for religious reasons, such as the case of tirthayatra (pilgrimage) in India.
