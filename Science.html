What is GSAT satellites?
The GSAT(GeosynchronosSatellite)are India”S indigenously developed communications satellites,used for digital audio,data and video brocasting.
What is the use of GSAT Satellites?
GSAT Satellites are communications satellites developed in India that are used for digital audio data and video broadcasting.
ISRO developed the GSAT series of geosynchronous satellites with the goal of making India self-sufficient in broadcasting services.

How do these satellites help in weather forecasting?
These Weather Satellites are an important observational tool for all scales of NWS forecasting operations.
